The Junk Wax Review...

If you are asking what "Junk Wax" let me take a moment to wax nostalgic...
The Junk Wax era was a dark period in the history or sport card collecting. It spans roughly from about 1988 to 1993. and althought it was short-lived, it almost killed the hobby. It also coinsides with the time I began collecting in earnest, starting with the 1991-92 Upper Deck Hockey series...
What you will find below is a review of (almost) every hockey set from that era. And while some look back on the Junk Wax era with distain, it hol;d fond memories for me, and more importantly, not all sets were "junk", many have stood the test of time to become little gems.
The sets & rookies form the era may not be helping put your kids through college, but there are still some nice, affordable sets left behind
