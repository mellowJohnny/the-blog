The last few years of O-Pee-Chee releases were a bit of a mixed bag in terms of production. Between 1982-83 and 1984-85 products was rumoured to be way up, with the '85-'85 set being the worst offender. Based on a contest they ran that year I calculate (very roughly) that they produced roughly the following:

  • Contest Odds: 1:662 of winning one of 10,000 prizes (mostly uncut sheets of cards)
  • Essentially every 662nd pack would spawn a winner x 10,000 = ~6 620 000 packs produced
  • With 15 cards in each pack = 993,000,000,000 total cards would have been printed
