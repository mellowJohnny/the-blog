Ah...the '90s
Miami Vice, those teal San Jose Sharks jerseys...and a hockey card hobby boom. Consider the 1989-90 season which featured exactly two main sets - Topps for the US market, and O-Pee-Chee for Canada. Three years later there were thirteen sets between US and Canada sets - 14 if you include the McDonald's set. That sheer volume of cards earned the era the "Junk Wax" moniker. But there are some hidden gems to be found if you look hard enough. So let's take a trip down memory lane and see what we discover...
