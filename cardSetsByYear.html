Ah...the late '80s / early '90s
Miami Vice, acid wash jeans, those teal San Jose Sharks jerseys...and a hockey card explosion. Pop quiz hot shot: how many hockey sets were available for the 1989-90 NHL season? Exactly two - Topps for the US market, and O-Pee-Chee for Canada. Just three years later there were thirteen (13!) sets available to US and Canadian collectors - sixteen if you include the French versions of the Upper Deck & Parkhurst sets, plus McDonald's. So you can see why, with the sheer volume of cards produced during these heady days, the era earned the Junk Wax moniker.

But there are some hidden gems to be found if you are willing to sift through the rubble. So let's take a trip down memory lane shall we?
