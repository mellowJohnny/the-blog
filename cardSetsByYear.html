Ah...the late '80s / early '90s...

Miami Vice, acid wash jeans, those teal San Jose Sharks jerseys...and a hockey card explosion. Pop quiz hot shot: how many hockey sets were produced for the 1989-90 NHL season? There were just two - Topps for the US and O-Pee-Chee for Canada. But just three years later there were no less than thirteen (!) sets available to US and Canadian collectors - not including French variations from Upper Deck & Parkhurst or the set from McDonald's. So you can see why, with the sheer volume of cards produced during these heady days, the era earned the Junk Wax moniker.

But there are some hidden gems to be found if you are willing to sift through the rubble. So let's start diggin'!
