...the wee little card checker

Let's say you have an incomplete set of sports cards but you want to begin adding to standard 9x9 UltraPro pages. First you figure out how many pages you need, and load a binder. Then you start adding in the cards....and counting empty slots to ensure the cards you do have are in the right slot for a given page.
But what if you mis-count those empty slots? Depending on where you miscounted, you later realise half the cards are now in the spot...and now all those cards need to be moved. One. By. One. Wouldn't it be great if you already knew exactly which slot a given card would occupy, regardless of the page?

Now you can!

Just enter the card number below, and find out exactly which slot to put the card in! It even tells you which page!
